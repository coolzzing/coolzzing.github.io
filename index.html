Sihyeon Park

'Vujà dé' is all about my perspective of both
Korean and American cultures.
I found ‘wishes, symbols, happiness, etc.’
in my new life in the U.S..
It was the 'Déjà vu' that I felt in 'Minhwa',
a traditional Korean folk art,
which is my painting inspiration.
Creativity comes from different viewpoints
like 'Vujà dé(a familiar but new feeling)'.
My pieces bring out familiar Korean and
American things in order to give people
a fresh feeling by combining representative
Korean paintings, symbolic images of the U.S.
and my own story.
This implies that, in the end,
our happiness is all the same.
